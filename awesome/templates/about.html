What is Project Awesome?
We built Project Awesome with two goals in mind:
  1. Define and spread your life's awesomeness.
  2. Share in the awesomeness of others.
Chats and status updates and cool and all.
But what is the personal meaning behind everything? What really matters to people in the world? What are people doing to think about and move towards their grand visions in life?
This is what Project Awesome is all about.
Getting Started
Getting started is simple.
Join and put together your own personal board that reflects your own life thoughts, goals, and ambitions. Browse other vision boards and join our amazing and inspirational community of people working on making their life and the world awesome!
Creating Visions
You can add a vision to your own board in three ways:
  1. Upload: Once you join, click the 'Add Vision' button in the top navigation bar. You will be prompted to upload an image of your choosing, as well as a text description of what this vision personally means to you.
  2. Repost: While browsing other visions, a 'Repost' button will appear when you hover your mouse over the vision. By clicking this button, you will automatically create your own copy of vision on your board.
  3. Post It bookmarklet: You can use our Post It bookmarklet to create a vision with a great picture on the web. For more information on how to install and use the bookmarklet, go here!
Your Vision Board
You may add up to 20 visions to your vision board. We put a limit here for a purpose: your vision board should be a focused representation of what you value and what matters to you.
Sharing with Others
Have friends on the site? Found awesome people you would like to connect with? You can follow them by simply going to their profile and clicking on the 'Follow' button! Keep track of all the awesomeness by clicking on the 'Friends' tab in the top navigation bar to see a feed of recent activity.
Play nice and be cool :)
Project Awesome is a community of people what have dreams, want the best for themselves, and the best for others.
Please be nice, stay support, and inspirational. It is easy to be negative and put down others but that does nothing good for the world. Don't be uncool.
