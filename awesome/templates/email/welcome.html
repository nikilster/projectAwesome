Welcome to the Project Awesome community!

The goal of Project Awesome is to bring out the total awesomeness of the world. This starts with your life, which you could consider your own personal Project Awesome. From there, it spreads out to everyone you interact with and reaches the world.

Here are some things that are good to know before getting started.

For your convenience, all of this information (and more!) is on our About page . But if you have a minute or two now, read on!

Getting started

Getting started is simple.

Build your personal vision board . A good vision board reflects your own life ideals, goals, and ambitions. Perhaps they are values you hold dear. Maybe they are your short- and long-term goals. Or your New Years resolutions. Take your time and think about it - a vision can consist of anything so be creative!

Engage with what matters in our life . You have an awesome vision board. Congrats! Now what? The best thing you can do is make it part of your life. Live it! Come back often to jot down your thoughts, or keep updates on your progress. We will help remind you with daily inspirational emails!

Share the love with others . Life can be good alone, but is always better with the right people. Project Awesome is a community of people who are passionate about living good lives, and improving the world. Take advantage of it! Browse other vision boards, connect with friends, and join our amazing community!

Creating visions

  1. Upload vision: If you are logged in, click on the 'Add Vision' button in the top navigation bar. It will bring up a dialog for creating a vision.
  2. Repost vision: While browsing the home page, or other people's vision board, you can copy a vision by clicking on the 'Repost' button.
  3. Postmarklet: If you find a an image you love online, you can use our postmarklet to create a vision with it. Go to the Postmarklet page for more information on how to install the postmarklet.

Sharing and privacy

Sharing is caring. A big part of our community revolves around people sharing their hopes, dreams, and general awesomeness. However, sometimes you want to keep a vision private. To do so, just click on a picture to zoom into its details, hover over the vision text, and click on the 'Edit' button to change the privacy setting.

You can also go to the Settings page to set the default privacy on your new visions.

Be nice and stay cool

Please be nice, supportive, and inspirational. It is easy to be negative and put down others, but that does nothing good for the world. Don't be uncool. Be awesome instead.

Enjoy!
