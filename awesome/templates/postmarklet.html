Post It Button
Drag this to your bookmarks bar.
To install and use the Post It button, follow these steps:
  1. Enable your bookmarks bar
    If you see bookmarks at the top of your browser window, you already have your bookmarks bar enabled. If not, go to the browser's view settings and enable the bookmarks bar.
  2. Install the Post It Button
    Put your mouse over the Post It Button above, click down, drag it into the bookmarks bar, and release the mouse button to drop the button into your bookmarks. Your browser may prompt you to change the name of the button. Leave it as 'Post It' or change it to whatever you would like and press OK. The Post It Button is now installed!
  3. Find and post visions
    When you come across an image you'd like for one of your visions, simply click on the Post It Button in your bookmarks bar!
